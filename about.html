China King is a family-owned restaurant located in Selmer TN. The owners, Lin Bao Hua and Wu Lin Hui, along with their children, David Wu and Kelly Wu, moved from New York in 2007 to take ownership of China King at the request of a family friend. Ever since, the China King family has become a staple in the Selmer community.
