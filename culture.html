China is one of the four ancient civilizations dated back to over 3000 years ago. Ancient China lasted c. 1600–221 BC. It was a time of growing and gaining different territories. After the unification of the many kingdoms, the Imperial age started. The Imperial age lasted from 221 BC – 1912 AD. This time period marks the bulk of Chinese history. With rise and falls of many dynasties, civilization was cultivated and prospered in times of peace. Then rebellions and conquests moved it into the next generation. The Republic of China was from 1912-1949. Since the republic could not establish well, it was decades of civil war battles. After the founding of the People's Republic of China in 1949, China has been in stability since.
There are 12 zodiac signs in Chinese culture. Each year represents a different animal. It is believed that each zodiac has its own personailty. They are also used to determine your capability with other signs when it comes to a relationship. Each zodiac also has a month, days of a week, and hours. These are used to determine a more in-depth destiny. Because of this insight, you can also predict your future with the signs. It is also said that when your birth year comes around every 12 years you will have bad luck. The 12 zodiacs are the rat, ox, tiger, rabbit, dragon, snake, horse, goat, monkey, rooster, dog, and pig.
Chinese food is very popular around the world. Different areas have different cuisine types: bland, sweet, salty, spicy, or sour. In China, the different regions have different styles of cooking. In Northern China, they use less vegatables and have wheat as their main,stable food. Foods such as noodles and dumplings are popular there. Western China has hearty halal food. Eastern China has sweet and light food. Central China has spicy with a lot of seasoning food. There are also Chinese foods that are used for medical purposes.
Tea is a very important part of Chinese culture. It is part of their daily lives and plays a great part in their economy and social lives. The growing of the teas has been perfected over the many generations. There are many types of tea, including green tea, black tea, Oolong tea, white tea, yellow tea, and dark tea. Here are some tips for drinking tea to have the best experience: drink it hot, do not drink to much strong tea, best time to drink is between meals, and do not drink with medication.
There are three main lucky colors in Chinese culture. They are red, green, and yellow. Each color represents different thing. For instance, red represents happiness, success, and good fortune, green represents money and wealth, and yellow represents royalty and power of the throne. Red is the most famous color in Chinese culture. It is used widely at occasions and weddings. The most important color is yellow. The reason why is because the first king was known as the Yellow King. The businesses and restaurants are usally painted in green. Also, the luckiest number in Chinese culture is the number 8.
